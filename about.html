About Us

PeregrinEduCare is committed to transforming education through innovative teaching models and modern techniques. Our mission is to empower both teachers and students with high-quality training, resources, and guidance that inspire lifelong learning and success.

PeregrineEduCare is a forward-thinking EdTech company dedicated to shaping the future of education. We focus on delivering innovative and technology-driven solutions that cater to diverse educational needs.

We specialize in providing comprehensive coaching programs that enhance skills, boost confidence, and prepare individuals to meet global educational standards. By integrating technology with proven learning methodologies, we create a dynamic and engaging learning environment.

Our expertise spans across multiple domains, including:

  • Comprehensive School Management Services
  • Support for CBSE and ICSE affiliations
  • Specialized coaching for NEET and IIT entrance examinations
  • Modern Library Setup and resource management
  • Organizing Olympiads at both national and state levels
  • Providing Skill Development Courses for schools & Colleges
