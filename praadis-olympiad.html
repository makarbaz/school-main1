Peregrine Olympiad (MAT)

The Peregrine Olympiad is an State level academic competition designed to identify and nurture exceptional talent across various disciplines. our Olympiad has grown to become one of the most prestigious academic competitions in the world.

Key Features:

  • Well recognized certification
  • Multiple difficulty levels for different age groups
  • Scholarships and monetary prizes for top performers
  • Comprehensive syllabus covering multiple subjects

Olympiad Structure

The competition is divided into multiple stages, each designed to test different aspects of academic excellence.

1

Level 1: Preliminary Round(School level)

School-level screening test covering fundamentals of Mathematics, Science, and Logical Reasoning.

Date: November 01 , 2025

2

Level 2: District level

State-level examination with advanced problems and application-based questions.

Date: December 06, 2025

3

Level 3: State Level

Final round with theoretical and practical challenges to determine the country's best minds.

Date: December 27, 2025

Olympiad Syllabus

The Olympiad syllabus for Classes 3rd to 5th includes Mathematics, Science, , and General Aptitude. For 6th To 8th includes Mathematics, Science ,English and General Aptitude . For Class 9thalso includes Science and Social Science ,English and General Aptitude subjects based on the NCERT Syllabus.

Class 3rd to 5th Syllabus

  • Mathematics (based on Class 5 NCERT)
  • English (basic grammar, vocabulary, comprehension)
  • Intelligence (logical reasoning, pattern recognition)
  • General Knowledge (basic Science, Social Science, current affairs)

Class 6th to 8th Syllabus

  • Mathematics (based on Class 5 NCERT)
  • English (basic grammar, vocabulary, comprehension)
  • Intelligence (logical reasoning, pattern recognition)
  • General Knowledge (basic Science, Social Science, current affairs)

Class 9 Syllabus

  • Mathematics (based on Class 8 NCERT)
  • English (advanced grammar, comprehension, writing)
  • Intelligence (logical and analytical reasoning)
  • General Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology from Class 8)
  • Social Science (History, Civics, Geography from Class 8)
